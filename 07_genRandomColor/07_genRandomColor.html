Random color genrater

Generate random colors and numbers from 1–50. If the number 25 appears in any box, its color spreads

to all boxes and the User wins. The User has 10 attempts per round. If the User fails to win within 10 attempts,

the Computer wins and Click New Game to start a fresh round and Click Reset Game to clear all scores and attempts.
